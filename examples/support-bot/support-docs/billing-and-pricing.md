# Billing and Pricing

## Plans

### Free Tier
- 1 space
- 100 documents
- 1,000 API calls per month
- Community support

### Pro Plan — $49/month
- 5 spaces
- 10,000 documents
- 100,000 API calls per month
- Email support (24-hour response time)
- Custom prompt templates

### Enterprise Plan — Custom pricing
- Unlimited spaces
- Unlimited documents
- Unlimited API calls
- Dedicated support with SLA
- SSO/SAML integration
- Custom deployment options
- Priority feature requests

## Billing Cycle

- All plans are billed monthly on the 1st of each month
- Your first bill is prorated from your signup date
- Invoices are sent to the billing email on your account

## Updating Payment Method

1. Go to Organization > Billing in the dashboard
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter your new credit card details
4. Click "Save" — your next invoice will use the new payment method

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and bank transfers (Enterprise only).

## Canceling Your Subscription

1. Go to Organization > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Plan"
3. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period
4. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation

## Refund Policy

- We offer a full refund within the first 14 days of a new subscription
- Refunds are not available for partial months after the 14-day period
- Contact support@tavora.ai for refund requests

## Usage Monitoring

Track your usage in the dashboard:
- Go to Space Settings > Token Usage to see API call counts
- Go to Space Settings > Metrics for detailed usage analytics
- You'll receive email alerts at 80% and 100% of your plan limits
